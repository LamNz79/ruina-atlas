# &#x20;                   **Zwei Association**

&#x09;				Civil protection and peacekeeping as a "shield" for clients



#### Trivia:

* The Zwei Association is named after the German word for the number two.



* This is reflected in their logo, with the Arabic numeral 2 being contained within the representation of a shield.



* This appears to line up with Faust's Germanic origins and placement as Limbus Company's second Sinner.



#### General:

* The Zwei Association handles requests relating to keeping peace in their assigned territories as well as providing single-target protection, referred to as "proximity protection".\[1] If necessary, they will provide covert supervision as not to disturb their clients' daily lives.\[2] During territory supervision, they will record notable observations.\[3] Fixers of the Zwei Association West Section are equipped to protect their assigned area for days on end, if necessary.\[4] According to Ishmael,\[5] while the South Section specializes in covert operations, the West Section values an imposing presence during their contracts.



* South Section 1 features a major crimes investigation unit.\[6]



* South Section's equipment consists of Zwei hander or Batons as well as protective navy-blue coats. West Section's equipment is reminiscent of knight armor.



##### Library of Ruina:

* The Zwei Association gets involved with the Library during Urban Legend, after nearly the entire Streetlight Office gets wiped out. San petitions his friends at the Association for help, who at first refuse, but later agree once the Library gets elevated from Urban Myth to Urban Legend Status. After San and Julia also get lost at the Library, Walter and Isadora go to investigate, eventually meeting the same fate as the others. Due to a previous secret agreement between the Stray Dogs and Walter, in which Walter was promised information on Distortions in exchange for a floor plan for the Nest of District 12, the news of Walter's death lead to the Stray Dogs investigating the Library as well.
