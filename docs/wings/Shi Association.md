# &#x09;	     **Shi Association**

&#x09;						     Assassinating targets



##### Trivia:

* The Shi Association is named after the Japanese word for the number four.



* This is reflected in their logo, with the part of the kanji highlighted in red appearing similar to the Arabic numeral 4.



* This appears to line up with Ryōshū's Japanese origins and placement as Limbus Company's fourth Sinner.



* The kanji 死 used for the Association's logo is likewise read as shi, and means death.



##### General:

* The Shi specialize in covert assassination requests. These types of requests tend to be quite common in the City, with many Fixers willing to take them up. This, accordingly, makes even a single Associate Fixer a costly endeavour. Shi Fixers stand out for their ability to complete them silently without leaving any witnesses. Shi Fixers will take on any target requested, ranging from regular civilians to dangerous combatants, as its members believe in equality in death. Given the nature of their work, there is an emphasis on client confidentiality. To handle each request undetected and within the time frame, Shi Fixers cultivate an eye for weak points and an ability to execute plans promptly. The difficulty of meeting these expectations leads the Shi to employ an overall lower number of Association Fixers than the other Associations. Its members similarly operate in untraditionally small groups.



* Each branch of the Shi Association uses different weapons, each suited for their environment. The Fixers of the Shi's southern branch wield long katanas, while the eastern branch's wield bowblades. The uniforms and weapons of the Shi Association tend to be black with red highlights.



* Following the events of Library of Ruina, its southern branch is still struggling to recover from the many casualties it faced, remaining at risk of being wiped out in its entirety.

##### Fighting Style:

* South Section Identities:

  * Similar to the Section 2 Fixers fought in Library of Ruina, Shi Association South Section 5 Identities gain buffs when they are at low health (specifically, less than 50% HP), with a secondary focus on **Poise** generation.
  * All Southern Shi Association Identities use primarily Slash Skills, with some Pierce Skills. All Identities additionally have **Envy**, **Lust**, and **Wrath** Affinity.



* East Section Identities:

  * Eastern Shi Association Identities revolve around **Arrow - Shi**, a resource similar to **Ammo**. They also generate **Poise** and inflict **Bleed**, being able to clash better the more **Poise** the user has and how much **Bleed** the target has. Additionally, their Skills are further strengthened based on the amount of **Arrow - Shi** spent, which can be renewed when the Lodged **Arrow** effect inflicted by a special Skill expires.
