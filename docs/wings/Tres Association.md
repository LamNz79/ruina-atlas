# &#x09;	    **Tres Association**

&#x09;					Managing Workshops and everything they produce



##### Trivia:

* The Tres Association is named after the Spanish word for the number three.
* This appears to line up with Don Quixote's Spanish origins and placement as Limbus Company's third Sinner.



General:

* The Tres Associations is responsible for managing Workshops all through the City. Their Fixers' duties include reviewing newly designed weapons, filing applications to the Head to register and patent the creations, and charging taxes relative to them. Their domain includes weapons, gadgets, and prosthetics models. When a design is rejected by the Association, it cannot be produced and distributed for commercial use in its current state, although it can still be kept for personal use. Upon rejection, the Association will at times send feedback suggesting how to improve the designs to a passable level.



* Like other Associations, Tres Association holds qualification exams for Fixers, including Workshop exams, presumably to obtain the necessary qualifications to run a Workshop.



* In recent years, the southern branch of Tres Association has suffered heavy losses due to Distortions, with the Blood-red Night and L'Heure du Loup as suspected guilty parties.
